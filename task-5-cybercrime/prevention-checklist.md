# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

### General Online Safety
1. Use strong, unique passwords for every account 
   and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
2. Never click on unknown links received via SMS, 
   WhatsApp or email
3. Keep your phone, apps and antivirus software 
   updated regularly
4. Do not share your OTP, password or personal 
   details with anyone — not even bank officials
5. Always verify the identity of the person 
   contacting you before sharing any information
6. Use only official and trusted websites for 
   shopping and transactions

### Financial / UPI Safety
7. Remember — UPI PIN is only entered to SEND 
   money, never to RECEIVE it. If someone asks 
   you to enter your PIN to receive money, 
   it is a SCAM.
8. Never scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers 
   online, especially on OLX or Facebook 
   Marketplace deals

### Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
- Report immediately if you suspect fraud — 
  quick reporting increases chances of recovery
