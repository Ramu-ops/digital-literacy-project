# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO's ✅

1. Keep your profiles professional and up to date
2. Share your academic achievements and projects
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts
4. Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd 
   be comfortable if your professor saw it
5. Give credit when sharing someone else's work
6. Use platforms like LinkedIn to network with 
   professionals in your field
7. Report cyberbullying or harassment immediately

## DON'Ts ❌

1. Never share your personal details like phone 
   number, address or passwords publicly
2. Don't post offensive, discriminatory or 
   inappropriate content
3. Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying
4. Don't share unverified news or fake information
5. Don't accept friend requests from unknown people
6. Don't post during exams or important academic 
   deadlines — stay focused!
7. Don't overshare your daily routine or location
